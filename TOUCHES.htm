Little Touches

“Little Touches” go a long way. Things like “dropcaps,” or having quotes be stylized, so the starting quote looks different than the closing one. (I do that with escape characters. It is subtle.) These little touches go a long way!

Yet another <aside> but with text on top and bottom. See, you don't have to use images for them. I also biggie-sized the font here through CSS magic. I feel confident using a dropcap cuz this is a big, chunky, block of text.

More tips on Asides:
  1. always have a grey text box underneath
  2. up top, they either have an image, or text yet again, but this time without the grey background.

Asides

  2. without an image

The zeppelin images is the first type (at the top of the page), and the one used here is the second type: Just text, baby!
